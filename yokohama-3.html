Area Guide
Yokohama

Yokohama grew up differently from Tokyo — shaped by its port and the foreign traders who settled here from the 1850s onward. That international character still runs through the city today: in its Chinatown (Japan's largest), its Western-style Yamate bluff houses, and its breezy waterfront feel. Only 30 minutes from central Tokyo but a completely different atmosphere.
