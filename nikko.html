Stop 01 · Shogun Sanctuary — Morning · 10:15 AM
UNESCO World Heritage · Edo Artistry

Toshogu Shrine Complex

日光東照宮
📍 View on Google Maps ↗

Step into the resting place of Tokugawa Ieyasu. Unlike the rustic simplicity of typical Japanese design, Toshogu features gold leaf work, vibrant color panels, and hundreds of custom animal reliefs. Terry details the hidden allegories behind the legendary Three Wise Monkeys and the Sleeping Cat.

💡 Terry steers you off the main paths up the stone stairs to the inner sanctum tomb, wrapped by centuries-old cryptomeria trees.
Stop 02 · Alpine Splendor — Afternoon · 02:00 PM
Highland Lake · Volcanic Waterfalls

Lake Chuzenji & Kegon Falls

中禅寺湖 · 華厳の滝
📍 View on Google Maps ↗

Ascend the famous winding Irohazaka highway into Oku-Nikko. Stand before Kegon Falls, a nearly 100-meter drop fed by the volcanic waters of scenic Lake Chuzenji. Walk along the lakeside piers to observe the historic diplomatic summer villas tucked into the tree line.

💡 Terry coordinates a private elevator trip down to the lower gorge base station, providing a perspective directly inside the roaring spray.
