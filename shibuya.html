Neon Crossroads · High Fashion Streets · Retro Enclaves

Shibuya is far more than a crowded intersection; it is a continuously evolving canvas of subcultures and architectural innovations. Terry takes you behind the towering video screens to navigate micro-boutiques in hidden alleys, explore elevated park developments, and share stories of the post-war shifts that turned this valley into an international trend capital.

Best For Contemporary streetwear fashion, vinyl music hunting, modern high-rise architecture, and culinary subcultures
Best Time to Visit Afternoon to evening — perfectly timed to experience the district's brilliant energy as the neon signs illuminate
