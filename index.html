Meet Terry
A familiar face in Japan with deep roots across the greater Tokyo area.
Terry B. Johnson
Local guide · Japan
Hi, I'm Terry! I've been living in Japan for 27 years, and over that time I've developed a deep knowledge and appreciation of Japanese culture, food, history, and everyday life. I love sharing both the famous sights and the hidden local spots that many visitors would never find on their own. Whether you're interested in traditional culture, modern city life, amazing food, local neighborhoods, or just experiencing Japan in a more relaxed and personal way, I'm here to help make your trip fun, comfortable, and memorable. I enjoy meeting people from all over the world and creating tours that feel friendly, informative, and easygoing. Looking forward to exploring Japan together!
