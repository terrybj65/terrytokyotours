Yokohama & Chinatown

Waterfront promenades, rich historic districts, and the incredible food paths of Japan's largest Chinatown.

Explore highlights ↓ Check availability
International Ports · Trading Bluffs · Culinary Streets

Yokohama has a personality completely its own — Japan's most international city, shaped by centuries of global trade and cultural intersection. Terry will guide you through the historic Yamate bluff where early foreign merchants lived, across the vibrant architecture of Chinatown, along the sleek Minato Mirai harborfront, and into timeless districts that seamlessly blend old and new.

Best For Waterfront strolls, global maritime history, vibrant street food, and modern architectural skylines
Best Time to Visit Late morning to evening — transitions beautifully from crisp daytime ocean breezes to dazzling neon harbor views

Yamate Bluff District

山手町
📍 View on Google Maps ↗

Ascend to the historic residential hillside overlooking the modern port city. Wander past beautiful 19th-century Western-style residences, elegant brick structures, and lush garden properties established when Japan first opened its doors to global trading merchants.

💡 The quiet neighborhoods up here feature elegant rose gardens that offer fantastic, uncrowded landscape frames of the bay.

Stop 02 · Culinary Hub — Mid-Day · 12:00 PM
Culinary Mainstays · Vibrant Landmarks

Yokohama Chinatown

横浜中華街
📍 View on Google Maps ↗

Step through monumental, ornately designed gates into Japan's largest Chinatown, home to over 600 diverse shops and dynamic restaurants. Take in the aromas of street food and explore lively cross-cultural paths filled with generations of local culinary legacy.

💡 Terry skips the modern commercial tourist traps to seat you at authentic, hidden local kitchens for lunch.
★★★★★ 4.5 · 24,000+ reviews
Stop 03 · Waterfront Stroll — Afternoon · 02:30 PM
Coastal Relaxation · Maritime Heritage

Yamashita Park

山下公園
📍 View on Google Maps ↗

Stroll down a spacious, beautiful waterfront promenade park that stretches directly along the active bay front. Watch modern ships navigate the harbor waters while enjoying refreshing sea breezes right next to historic vessels like the docked Hikawa Maru liner.

💡 The park is an ideal location to experience a peaceful moment away from the mid-day urban bustle.
★★★★★ 4.3 · 9,800+ reviews
Stop 04 · Modern Harbor — Evening · 05:00 PM
Modern Landscapes · Architectural Feats

Minato Mirai Waterfront

みなとみらい21
📍 View on Google Maps ↗

Conclude your journey amid the striking, state-of-the-art skyline profile of the "Harbor of the Future." Admire massive high-rise architectural marvels, repurposed historic red-brick storehouses, and the vibrant neon display of the iconic Cosmo Clock Ferris wheel.

💡 Watching the architectural harbor lights turn on at dusk provides a breathtaking finale to the day.
★★★★★ 4.6 · 15,000+ reviews
