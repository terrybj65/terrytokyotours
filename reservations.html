Cancellations by Terry

✅
If Terry has to cancel due to illness, emergency, or severe weather, you will receive a full 100% refund — or the option to reschedule at no extra cost.

Rescheduling

If you need to change your tour date, Terry will do his best to accommodate you. Rescheduling requests made 7 or more days before the tour are free of charge. Changes within 7 days are subject to availability and may incur the cancellation fee for the original date.

No-shows

⚠️
Customers who do not appear at the meeting point without prior notice will be treated as a same-day cancellation. No refund will be issued.

How refunds are processed

All refunds are returned via PayPal to the original payment method within 5–7 business days of the cancellation being confirmed by Terry.

Additional policies

Group size

Tours are private and limited to a maximum of 6 guests. This keeps the experience personal and ensures Terry can give everyone proper attention.

Children

Children are welcome on all tours. Please mention ages when booking — Terry will tailor the pace and content accordingly. Children under 5 are free of charge.

Accessibility

Please mention any mobility requirements or accessibility needs in your booking request. Terry will plan a route that works for everyone in your group.

Weather

Tours run in most weather conditions. Japan's seasons are part of the experience. In cases of typhoons or extreme weather warnings issued by the Japan Meteorological Agency, Terry reserves the right to cancel with a full refund.

Meeting point

Full meeting point details, a map link, and what to bring will be sent with your booking confirmation. If you are unsure how to get there, Terry is always happy to help navigate.

Photography

Terry is happy to take photos for you throughout the day. Please respect local rules at temples and shrines — some areas prohibit photography and Terry will advise on the spot.

Liability

Terry provides guidance and local knowledge. Customers participate in all activities at their own risk. Terry is not responsible for lost property, personal injury, or third-party costs (transport, entry fees, food) incurred during the tour.
