Area Guide
Hakone

Hakone is where Tokyo goes to exhale. A volcanic mountain resort in Kanagawa, it offers the best accessible views of Mt Fuji, traditional ryokan inn culture, open-air hot spring baths, and a stunning national park. Terry can help plan an overnight stay or a full-day loop on the famous Romancecar train, ropeway, and pirate ship across Lake Ashi.
