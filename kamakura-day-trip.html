About this tour
Kamakura Day Trip

Kamakura is everything Tokyo is not — quiet, forested, ancient. Just an hour by train, it feels like a different world. Terry will guide you through the major temples at the right time of day (before the crowds or after them), along the beautiful Daibutsu Hiking Trail through cedar forests, and down to the coast for fresh seafood. A perfect day out of the city.
